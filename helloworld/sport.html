My favorite sport is soccer.

Soccer is an intense sport of two teams with eleven players each. Each team aims to win by scoring the most goals with a spherical ball. The game is played on a rectangular field with a netted goal on each end using only their feet, legs, and head, players must maneuver the ball around their opponents to the other end of the field and score a goal.

I love soccer because it requires skill, teamwork, and strategy. Due to the long halves, the game is physically demanding. The position that I play most frequently is center midfield. Out of all the positions, this one requires the most endurance.
